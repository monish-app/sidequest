SIDE
QUEST

A daily dare app. One quest a day, pulled from a deck. Fun, slightly unhinged, worth a story. Do it, prove it, AI verifies, you earn points.

FORMULA: Truth-or-Dare × BeReal × Strava. Prompted, verified, social, a little chaotic.

See The Core Loop What Ships in V1
01

What It Is

Every day, you get one quest from a deck. You have until midnight to complete it, upload photo or video proof, and let AI verify it. Points, streaks, leaderboards, and squads turn it into a daily ritual.

The Deck
One quest, daily

Drops at a set time each day. No endless feed, just a single prompt that pushes you into a story.

The Proof
Verified, not vibes

Photo or video evidence. AI does first-pass verification; community review covers edge cases.

The Squad
Social by default

Proofs auto-post to your squad chat. Streaks and dual leaderboards keep everyone in the chase.

02

The Core Loop

1

Drop

A quest drops at a set time each day, like 9 AM local. You see its difficulty before accepting.

2

Complete

You have until midnight to go do it in the real world.

3

Prove

Upload a photo or short video as proof. One screen, two taps.

4

Verify

AI verifies the submission. Ambiguous cases route to community voting.

5

Score

Points are awarded by difficulty: 1x, 3x, or 5x.

6

Climb

Streak extends. Leaderboards update. The squad chat lights up.

Key Features

Daily Quest Drop

One quest per day from a deck you've unlocked. Free users get the standard deck; premium expands the themes.

AI Verification

Photo/video proof, AI first-pass, community fallback for ambiguous cases. Hard quests lean more on community review.

Skip Tokens

3 free skips on signup to keep a streak alive. Extra skips are paid or bundled into premium.

Streaks

Visible counter with celebratory milestones. The core retention loop.

Dual Leaderboards

Streak length and points (weekly + all-time), each at global, friend, and squad levels.

Squads

Private groups with friends, group chat where proofs auto-post, and a squad-only leaderboard.

06

Who It's For

Friend Groups

Squads who already have a group chat and a competitive streak. Side Quest gives the chat a daily reason to go off.

The Bored & Curious

People stuck in the scroll who want a nudge into something real. Small adventures, not another feed.

Streak Chasers

The Strava / Duolingo type, wired by stats, ranks, and not breaking the chain.

Story Collectors

People who want a better answer to "what did you do today" than nothing. Every quest is a story.

Monetization

Free · Ad-Supported
The full loop

Daily standard-deck quest, proof + AI verification, 3 skips, squads, leaderboards, streaks. Ads between quest reveal, after submission, and in the feed.

Ad-free + more

À la carte
